Our Original Story

Named "QUINTETETTE" after my beautiful granddaughters. The inspiration for doing a jewellery business came from my daughter-in-law, who is a homemaker and have an emotional attachment to jewellery. She believes jewellery can be a tangible reminder of those adventures, weddings, and other memories associated with them. QUINTETETTE is a fine jewellery company that creates jewellery that is an experience worthy of the moments it marks. Each piece tells a story. Each experience is memorable
